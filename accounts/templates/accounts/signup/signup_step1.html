Privacy Policy

What data we collect

We collect: email, name, degree program, graduation year, and optional bio.

How we use your data

To provide the TOSSR platform functionality - allowing you to share and access study materials.

Your rights

  • Access: Download your data anytime
  • Rectification: Edit your profile
  • Erasure: Delete your account
  • Portability: Export your data as JSON

Data retention

When you delete your account:

  • Your personal information (name, email, bio) is anonymized
  • Your account is deactivated and you cannot log in
  • Your published study materials and comments remain visible but show as "Deleted User"
  • This ensures other students can continue to benefit from resources you shared
